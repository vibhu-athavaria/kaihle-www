The Full Walkthrough

From diagnostic to personalised
learning — in one platform.

Kaihle brings together diagnostic assessment, student learning profiles, AI-generated content, and teacher tools — all mapped to your Cambridge or IB curriculum from day one. Here's exactly how it works.

01

Student takes a diagnostic assessment

When a student joins a class, Kaihle automatically creates a Tier 1 diagnostic — a short, curriculum-aligned assessment covering all major topics for their grade and subject. The student completes it once. The platform does the rest.

Teachers can also assign Tier 2 assessments at any point — before a new unit, mid-term, or after a period of absence.

02

A Learning Profile is built for every student

As students complete assessments and work through their study plans, Kaihle builds a rich Learning Profile for each one. This profile captures their strengths, the areas where they consistently struggle, how they prefer to learn, and how quickly they progress on different topic types.

The Learning Profile is not a static report — it evolves continuously. Every completed activity, quiz result, and study session adds to it. The AI uses this profile to personalise every single lesson and recommendation specifically for that student.

03

Kaihle maps the gaps

Every response is scored and mapped to a specific curriculum subtopic. Kaihle calculates a mastery score per subtopic for every student — and compares it across the class.

The teacher sees a colour-coded heatmap: green for strong, amber for developing, red for needs attention. Not averages. Not letter grades. Exact gaps, by subtopic, per student.

03

A personalised study plan is generated

For every gap identified, Kaihle generates a study plan. This includes curated external resources matched to the student's learning profile, and an AI-generated practice quiz to reinforce the concept.

Resources are selected by semantic similarity to the curriculum subtopic — not keyword search. Quality over quantity.

06

Teachers generate lesson plans with AI

Kaihle doesn't just help students — it transforms how teachers work. The Teacher AI Lesson Planner generates full lesson plans for any subject, topic, and year group at the click of a button. Each plan is automatically tailored to the class's current gap map, so teachers don't need to guess what to focus on.

Teachers review, customise, and teach. The hours previously spent creating assessments, planning lessons, and marking gaps can now be spent where it matters most: mentoring students, building relationships, and doing the work only a human teacher can do.
