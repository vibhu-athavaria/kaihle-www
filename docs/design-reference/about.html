Our Story

Built for schools that
care about every student.

Kaihle was built by people who spent time in classrooms and understood a frustrating truth: teachers are stretched, and existing tools weren't built to help them.

We started with a question: why do teachers still rely on gut instinct to know which students need help, and with what? In an era of AI, there had to be a better way.

The answer wasn't to build another LMS, or another quiz platform, or another flashcard app. It was to start with something different: a structured diagnostic that maps exactly what each student knows.

Everything Kaihle does — the gap heatmap, the personalised study plans, the AI-generated content — flows from that first insight. You can't fix what you haven't found.

We're building Kaihle working closely with micro schools and small international schools across Southeast Asia who use Cambridge and IB curricula. These are schools where every student matters, and where the gap between what students need and what they're getting is the widest.

What We Believe

The values behind every decision we make.

🎯

Diagnose before prescribing

We never generate a learning resource without first understanding what the student actually needs. Every study plan starts with a gap, not a guess.

🏫

Schools are the unit of change

Individual tools change one student's experience. A platform embedded in a school changes the experience for every student. That's where we focus.

🔍

Transparency over mystification

Teachers should always understand why Kaihle is showing them something. No black boxes. No "the AI said so." Every insight has a traceable source.

🌏

Built for this region

Most edtech is built for the US or UK market and adapted for everyone else. Kaihle is built for Southeast Asian schools first — and only expanding when the fit is real.

Close to Our Schools.

We work closely with the micro schools and small international schools we're building for. Every feature is informed by real conversations with teachers and school administrators in our target markets.
