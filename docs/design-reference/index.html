The Platform

Everything your school needs
to unlock better learning.

📋

Diagnostic Assessment

Curriculum-aligned assessments that map every student's knowledge gaps to specific learning objectives — automatically, at enrolment and throughout the year.

🧠

Student Learning Profiles

Kaihle builds a rich learning profile for every student — capturing their strengths, learning style, and progress patterns. The AI uses this to personalise every lesson specifically for them.

🗺️

Real-Time Gap Map

Teachers see a colour-coded heatmap of their entire class. Instantly know who is struggling, on what topic, and by how much. No spreadsheets. No guesswork.

✨

Personalised AI Lessons

For every gap identified, Kaihle generates a personalised study plan with AI-produced explainer content, animations, and practice tailored to how each student learns best.

📝

Teacher AI Lesson Planner

Teachers generate complete, gap-aware lesson plans in minutes. Spend less time creating resources and more time mentoring, coaching, and connecting with students.

🏫

School-Wide Analytics

Principals and heads of school see learning outcomes across every class. Identify where the school excels and where attention is needed — backed by real data, not gut feel.

How it works

From diagnostic to personalised learning — in three steps.
