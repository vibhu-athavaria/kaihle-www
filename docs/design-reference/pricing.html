Frequently Asked Questions

Pricing questions, answered.

How does Pay As You Go billing work?

You are billed monthly for each active student and teacher on the platform. If a user is inactive in a given month, they are not charged. Add or remove users at any time — your bill adjusts immediately.

Is the free trial really free? No credit card?

Yes, completely free for 30 days. No credit card, no commitment. You get full access to every feature — including Student Learning Profiles and the Teacher AI Lesson Planner. At the end of the trial, you decide whether to continue.

What curricula does Kaihle support?

Kaihle is built natively around Cambridge Primary & Secondary and IB curricula. Every diagnostic, gap map, and AI-generated lesson is already aligned to these frameworks out of the box — no setup required.

Can we bring our own custom curriculum?

Yes. Custom curriculum mapping is available for schools with their own programmes or local national curricula. This requires a dedicated onboarding engagement and is priced based on scope. Talk to us about custom curriculum →

Can we move from the free trial to paid without losing data?

Yes. All student records, learning profiles, gap history, and lesson plans carry over automatically. Nothing is lost when you move to a paid subscription.

Do you offer discounts for small or low-income schools?

Yes. We consider each situation individually. If budget is a genuine constraint but Kaihle is the right fit for your students, reach out — we want to find a way to make it work.
