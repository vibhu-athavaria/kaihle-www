Overview

Kaihle is a B2B platform. We work with schools, not individual families. We do not sell user data. We do not use advertising. Student data belongs to the school that enrolled them.

This Privacy Policy explains how Kaihle ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our platform or visit our website at kaihle.com.

By using Kaihle, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

What We Collect

For schools and administrators:

  • Name and email address of school administrators and teachers
  • School name, location, and curriculum type
  • Billing information (processed by Stripe — we do not store card details)

For students:

  • Name and grade level (provided by the school)
  • Assessment responses and scores
  • Learning progress and study plan completion data

For website visitors:

  • Demo request form submissions (name, email, school, role)
  • Anonymous analytics via Plausible (cookieless — no personal data)

How We Use It

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and improve the Kaihle platform
  • Generate diagnostic results, gap maps, and personalised study plans
  • Send weekly parent reports on behalf of the school
  • Respond to demo requests and support enquiries
  • Send transactional emails (e.g. account creation, billing receipts)

We do not use student data to train AI models or for any purpose beyond providing the Kaihle service to the school.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes. We use a limited number of trusted sub-processors:

  • Google Gemini API — AI content generation (assessment grading, study plan content)
  • AWS S3 — Secure media file storage (ap-southeast-1 region)
  • Resend — Transactional email delivery
  • Stripe — Payment processing

All sub-processors are bound by data processing agreements. Student data is never passed to sub-processors in identifiable form where avoidable.

Student Data

We take student data seriously. Kaihle operates as a data processor on behalf of the school, which acts as the data controller. This means:

  • The school controls what student data is entered into Kaihle
  • The school is responsible for obtaining appropriate consent from parents and guardians
  • Kaihle will not share student data with anyone except as directed by the school
  • Schools can request deletion of all student data at any time
Student data is stored in a separate, isolated database partition per school (multi-tenant isolation). No student's data is accessible to any other school's users or administrators.

Data Retention

We retain data for as long as your school has an active Kaihle account. When an account is closed:

  • All student data is deleted within 30 days of account closure
  • Billing records are retained for 7 years as required by law
  • Anonymous, aggregated analytics data may be retained indefinitely

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing

To exercise any of these rights, please email [email protected]. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We implement industry-standard security practices including:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
  • Data encrypted at rest on AWS infrastructure
  • Role-based access controls — school staff can only see their own school's data
  • Regular security reviews and dependency updates
